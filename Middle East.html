Why North America?

The region is the world's largest consumer market, and has the highest rate of consumption as a percentage of the GDP.
This means not only are people spending lots on buying things, they are also using more of their paycheque than any other region in the world.
Relatively lax customs and considerably efficient shipping rates, along with strong distributers and wholesalers, make North America the closest place to an ideal exporting region.

Why Middle-East?

The Middle-Eastern Market presents numerous opportunities. Aside from a small number of exceptions, Middle-Eastern Countries have a relatively high GDP per Capita, and very little unemployment(e.g. Unemployment rate in Qatar is around 0.50%).
Furthermore, with the next World Cup(2022) being in Qatar, Middle-Eastern Demand for outside products will soon increase exceptionally.
